About Us

The British rock band's members have included Freddie Mercury, Brian May, Roger Taylor, and John Deacon and are one of the biggest bands in the world. The members of Queen had an insatiable appetite for songwriting and performing. Attention to the finest details is what set this band apart from other acts of the time. The list of unique and timeless songs they created are endless, including, "Bohemian Rhapsody","We Will Rock You","We Are The Champions","Somebody To Love" and many others. Similar kinds of creativity, art, energy and emotions in their music and performances proves the legendality of the rock band "Queen".
